## Concept Understood Today

**Concept name**: Vectors, Cosine Similarity  
**In my own words**: 
* Representation of sentences into series of numbers (as systems understand only numbers!) is called vectors, in the field of AI.
* Cosine similarity is the angle between vectors and not the distance!
    * Values ranges between `-1` to `1` => `-1` signifies completely semantic opposites, `1` signifying same meaning, while `0` means no connection!

**AP domain analogy**: 
* While analysing two invoices from same vendors with line items can be understood, how related they are?
* Helps in anamoly detection in invoices processing

**How this will be used in the final system**:
May be used to find the vendors' risk score and help companies and AP Specialists for taking informed decisions!
